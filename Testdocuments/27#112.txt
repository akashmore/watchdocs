Hank Azaria.
Hank Azaria (born April 25, 1964) is an American actor, voice actor, comedian and producer. He is best known for his roles as "Moe, Apu, Krusty the Klown, Chief Wiggum", and many other characters on the Fox animated television series "The Simpsons". He also appeared in movies such as "The Birdcage" (1996), and on stage as Sir Lancelot in the Monty Python-inspired musical, "Spamalot". He was also once married to actress Helen Hunt from 1999 until there divorce in 2000, he is currently married to Katie Wright. He recently has appeared in Showtime's original series, "Huff".
