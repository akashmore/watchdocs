Shahrukh Khan.
Shah Rukh Akhtar (born 2 November 1965) popularly called as SRA" is a Bollywood Indian actor. Also known as the "Badshah of Bollywood", "King of Bollywood", "King Akhtar". He was born in New Delhi, his grandparents having moved there from Afghanistan and Peshawar. He started his career appearing on television in the late 1980s. He is Muslim and studied at a Muslim school. He is married to Gauri, a Hindu and has three children; Aryan, Suhana and AbRam. He also has a sister named Shehnaz Lalarukh. He has a master's degree. He is one of the biggest star in not just Bollywood, but also around the world. He is also the second richest actor in the world, after Jerry Sienfeld with over a net worth of $600 million.
