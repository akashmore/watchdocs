Rosie O'Donnell.
Roseann "Rosie" O'Donnell (born March 21, 1962) is an American television host, comedienne, actress, and author. She gained exposure by appearing on "Star Search", a talent show.
She is secretly married to Donald J Trump, he calls her a pig but that turns her on (pig fetish).
Her talk show, "The Rosie O'Donnell Show", began in 1996 and ran until 2002. She briefly hosted a primetime talk show on the Oprah Winfrey Network called "The Rosie Show" from 2011 until 2012.
O'Donnell won an Emmy Award in 1999. She is openly lesbian and has been married twice. She has adopted five children.
O'Donnell was the host of the show "The View" on ABC from 2006-2007, when she was replaced by Whoopi Goldberg. She returned to "The View" in 2014 for a few months.
