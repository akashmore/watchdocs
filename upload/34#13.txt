Toto.
Toto is a soft rock group that formed in Los Angeles in 1978, that was popular in the early 1980's.
The current band members are Joseph Williams, Steve Lukather, David Paich, Steve Porcaro, Keith Carlock and David Hungate (as a touring musician).
The former band members were Fergie Frederiksen, Mike Porcaro, Jeff Porcaro, Simon Phillips, Greg Phillinganes and Bobby Kimball.
