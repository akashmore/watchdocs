Phil Collins.
Philip David Charles "Phil" Collins (born 30 January 1951) is an English singer, drummer and actor. He was born in Chiswick, Hounslow, Middlesex. He plays with the band Genesis. He is very well known for his career away from the band, as a solo singer. His most well known album is "No Jacket Required", which has sold over ten million copies in the United States. One of his most well known songs is "In the Air Tonight", which had a long running false rumour that it was about a drowning that Collins had witnessed. Collins starred in the movie "Buster" (1988).
Collins won the Academy Award for Best Original Song and the Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song for "You'll Be in My Heart". That song was in the movie "Tarzan".
